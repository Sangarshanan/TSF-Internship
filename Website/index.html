What are we?

The Sparks Foundation is a charitable organization running in India and Singapore, and expanding. Our mission is to inspire students, let them integrate and help them innovate to build a better humankind.

How old is The Sparks Foundation?

The Sparks Foundation is a startup. We started with basic charity endeavors under different names in the education sector. About a year ago, we launched a full spectrum of our operations under the formal name of The Sparks Foundation.

Where is The Sparks Foundation located?

Currently we have registered offices in New Delhi and Singapore. The Sparks Foundation is operational in India and Singapore at the moment, however, we operate virtually worldwide. We will register offices in Philippines and USA in FY 2018.
